Rules of the Games:

- The computer selects a secret word.

- The computer shows the length of the word with underscores.

- You guess one letter at a time.

- The computer reveals correct guesses;

- otherwise, it tracks incorrect guesses.

- There's a limit for 6 incorrect guesses.

- You win by guessing the word within the limit,

or you lose if you exceed it.

- Have fun trying to guess the word!
