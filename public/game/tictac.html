Game Description

Welcome to the game of Tic-Tac-Toe! This classic two-player game challenges your strategy and wit. Before you start, here are the rules you need to know.
Objective:
The objective of Tic-Tac-Toe is to be the first player to get three of your symbols in a row on the 3x3 grid.
Game Setup:
1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid, and it starts with an empty board.
2. You will be "X," and the computer will be "O." "X" always goes first.
Game Rounds:
1. This game is played in a series of three rounds.
2. To be declared the overall winner, you need to win 2 out of the 3 rounds.
Gameplay:
1. You take turns to make your moves, starting as "X."
2. A move consists of selecting an empty cell on the grid and marking it with your symbol ("X").
3. The computer will make its moves as "O."
Winning the Round:
To win a round, you must achieve one of the following:
* Get three of your symbols in a horizontal row (across).
* Get three of your symbols in a vertical row (up or down).
* Get three of your symbols in a diagonal row (from top-left to bottom-right or from top-right to bottom-left).
